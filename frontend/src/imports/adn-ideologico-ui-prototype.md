Create a clean, minimalist UI prototype for a web app called “ADN Ideológico”.

The website is an interactive political self-discovery test that helps users understand how they think ideologically beyond the traditional left vs right labels.

The design should feel neutral, thoughtful and modern, similar to a psychological self-assessment tool.

STYLE
- Minimalist
- White background
- Soft neutral colors with one accent color (blue or purple)
- Clean typography (modern sans-serif)
- Subtle and simple illustrations related to data or thinking
- Calm, trustworthy and curiosity-driven tone
- Not partisan or political-looking

DEVICES
Design responsive layouts for:
- Mobile
- Desktop

STRUCTURE OF THE WEBSITE

1. LANDING PAGE

Hero section:
Title:
"¿Seguro que sabes cómo piensas políticamente?"

Subtitle:
"La mayoría de las personas se definen como izquierda o derecha.  
Pero cuando analizamos valores reales, el resultado suele ser diferente."

Primary button:
"Descubrir mi ADN ideológico"

Secondary button:
"Ver ejemplo"

Include a simple illustration related to thinking, data mapping, or mental maps.

Below hero:
Short explanation section:

"Este test analiza diferentes dimensiones de tus valores sociales, económicos y culturales para generar un mapa ideológico personal."

Show a preview radar graphic illustration as a visual hint of the result.

---

2. TEST SCREEN (QUESTION FLOW)

Show a clean test interface.

Top:
Progress indicator

Example:
Pregunta 1 de 5

Center:
Large question text.

Example question:
"El Estado debería intervenir más en la economía para reducir desigualdades."

Answer scale:

1 2 3 4 5 6 7

Left label:
"Muy en desacuerdo"

Right label:
"Muy de acuerdo"

Navigation:
- Siguiente
- Volver

Keep layout extremely clean and focused.

---

3. LOADING / ANALYZING SCREEN

After finishing questions show a short analysis screen.

Title:
"Analizando tu ADN ideológico..."

Animated checklist style elements:

✔ valores económicos  
✔ libertades civiles  
✔ autoridad y estado  
✔ tecnología y sociedad  
✔ globalización  
✔ sostenibilidad

Include a subtle animation placeholder.

---

4. RESULTS SCREEN

Title:
"Tu ADN ideológico"

Display a radar chart with 6 axes:

- Economía
- Libertades civiles
- Autoridad
- Ecología
- Globalización
- Tecnología

Under the radar chart show a short placeholder description text.

Example:

"Tu perfil combina una visión económica moderadamente social con una fuerte preferencia por libertades civiles y apertura tecnológica."

Buttons:

Primary:
"Compartir mi perfil"

Secondary:
"Repetir test"

---

5. SHARE SCREEN

Title:
"Comparte tu ADN ideológico"

Show a preview card/image that users could share.

Include:

- Radar chart
- Title: ADN Ideológico
- Subtitle: "Descubre cómo piensas realmente"

Share buttons:

WhatsApp  
X / Twitter  
Instagram (download image)  
Copiar enlace

Below share buttons include a subtle secondary button:

"Cuéntanos si te esperabas este resultado"

---

6. FEEDBACK FORM

Simple minimal form.

Title:
"¿Te esperabas este perfil?"

Subtitle:
"Tu opinión nos ayuda a mejorar el test."

Single text field:

Placeholder:
"Cuéntanos qué te ha sorprendido de tu resultado..."

Buttons:

Enviar  
Volver al inicio

Keep the form very simple and low pressure.

---

GENERAL DESIGN NOTES

- Avoid partisan colors (no red/blue political cues)
- Focus on curiosity and introspection
- Use simple data visualization style
- Radar chart should be visually attractive
- Maintain generous whitespace
- Design should feel trustworthy and reflective

Create a full UI flow with connected screens that can be used as an interactive prototype.
